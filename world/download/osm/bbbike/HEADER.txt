OSM extracts of more than 200 cities and regions world wide. 
------------------------------------------------------------

The OSM extracts are 2-50 MB large for each city or area.

Are you missing an city or area? Please go to 

  http://www.bbbike.org/cgi/extract.cgi

and select your own area!
