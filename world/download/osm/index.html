This download site offers a database dump of the OpenStreetMap Full Planet in OSM XML file format and Protocolbuffer Binary Format. Also available are extracts of more than 200 cities and regions world wide.

  • Full Planet, in PBF (11.8GB) or OSM XML (24GB) format
  • more than 200 cities and regions world wide, 2-50MB in size for each city
  • select your own region, a rectangle up to 6000 x 4000km large

All Data from OpenStreetMap is licensed under the OpenStreetMap License. Excerpts on this page are created and updated by BBBike.org. All times are given as CE(S)T. The files will be updated once a week on Thursday evening.
